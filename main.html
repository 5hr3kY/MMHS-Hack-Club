Hack Club is a national programming club made by teens, for teens. At Murrieta Mesa, our hack club is meant for any student interested in computer science! Hacking is a term used for creation, not just intercepting security protocols (silly).

It was made for creating a community in Mesa interested in programming! No experience is required at all to join the club. There'll be a lot of resources to learn from during workshops and hakcathons! You'll gain rewards after every project you create!

Scroll down to see more about what happens in our club!

Meetings

Meetings are weekly and generally chill! Bring your lunch while we discuss upcoming workshops and hackathons.

Workshops

These are normally afterschool and are on a monthly to bi-monthly occurance. You'll learn how to program with a specific topic in mind and gain a prize after completing the project.

hackathons

Hackathons are normally a 1-2 day event on weekends where we will travel to a different school. You will be working in groups to complete a specific project. there will be free workshops, swag, food, and prizes at the hackathon.
